Frequently Asked Questions

How do I submit my own recipe to Foodie Faves?

We love receiving recipe submissions from our community! To submit your recipe, click on the "Upload Recipe" button in the top navigation. You'll need to create a free account if you don't already have one. Our editorial team reviews all submissions within 5-7 business days. Make sure to include clear instructions, ingredient measurements, and high-quality photos to increase your chances of being featured.

What's the best way to contact customer support?

For fastest response, use our contact form above or email support@foodiefaves.com. Our support team typically responds within 24 hours Monday-Friday. If you're experiencing technical issues with the website or app, please include screenshots and details about your device/browser. For account-related questions, have your username or email address ready.

How can I report an error in a recipe?

We appreciate when our community helps us maintain recipe accuracy. On each recipe page, you'll find a "Report Error" button below the instructions. Alternatively, you can email corrections@foodiefaves.com with the recipe title, specific issue, and your suggested correction. Our test kitchen will verify the issue and update the recipe accordingly.

Do you offer cooking classes or culinary events?

Yes! We host monthly virtual cooking classes featuring our most popular recipes and seasonal specials. In-person events are held quarterly at our Brooklyn test kitchen. Check our Events page for upcoming schedules and ticketing information. We also offer private virtual classes for groups - email events@foodiefaves.com for more information.

How do I request permission to use your recipes in my publication?

All recipe content is copyrighted by Foodie Faves. For reprint permissions, please contact licensing@foodiefaves.com with details about your publication, circulation, and which recipes you'd like to use. Most individual home cooks are welcome to use our recipes with proper attribution without needing formal permission. Our full copyright policy is available on our Terms page.

More Ways to Connect

We actively monitor our social media channels and typically respond within a few hours. DM us on Instagram @FoodieFavesOfficial or tweet @FoodieFavesHelp for quick questions. Our social team can assist with account issues, recipe questions, and general inquiries. For privacy concerns, we recommend using email instead.

2. Community Forums

Join our active community of home cooks in the Foodie Faves forums. While not official support, many experienced members can help with recipe troubleshooting, substitutions, and technique questions. 3. Live Chat (Premium Members)

Premium members have access to our live chat feature during business hours. Connect instantly with our culinary team for real-time cooking advice, recipe modifications for dietary restrictions, or technical support. Look for the chat icon in the bottom right corner when logged into your premium account.

4. Newsletter Feedback

Each edition of our weekly newsletter includes a "Reply to this email" option. We read every response and typically reply within 48 hours. 5. Video Comments

For questions about our recipe videos, leave a comment on the video page. Our video producers and culinary team monitor comments and will respond to questions about techniques, ingredients, or preparation shown in the videos. Comments are also a great place to share your results or variations you've tried.

6. Affiliate Program Inquiries

Bloggers, influencers, and publishers interested in our affiliate program should email affiliates@foodiefaves.com. Include information about your platform, audience size, and why you'd be a good fit. Our partnerships team reviews applications weekly and typically responds within 7-10 business days.

7. Press & Media

Journalists and media professionals can contact press@foodiefaves.com for interview requests, press kits, or expert commentary.
