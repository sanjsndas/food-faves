Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 15, 2023

Introduction

At Foodie Faves, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services. By accessing or using our platform, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

1. Personal Identification Information

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide when you register for an account, such as your name, email address, username, and password. This information is essential for creating and managing your account, personalizing your experience, and providing customer support.

2. Profile Information

You may choose to provide additional information for your public profile, including a profile picture, biography, location, dietary preferences, and cooking skill level. This information helps other users get to know you and allows us to personalize recipe recommendations.

3. Recipe and Content Submissions

When you submit recipes, comments, reviews, or other content to our platform, we collect and store this information. This includes recipe details, photos, cooking instructions, nutritional information, and any other content you choose to share with the community.

4. Usage Data

We automatically collect information about how you interact with our website, including pages visited, time spent on pages, recipes viewed, searches performed, and features used. This helps us understand user behavior and improve our services.

5. Device and Technical Information

We collect technical data such as your IP address, browser type, operating system, device information, and mobile network information. This helps us optimize our platform for different devices and troubleshoot technical issues.

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies, web beacons, and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience, analyze trends, administer the website, track movements around the site, and gather demographic information. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

7. Payment Information

For premium services, we collect payment information such as credit card details, billing address, and transaction history. All payment transactions are processed through secure third-party payment processors, and we do not store full credit card numbers.

8. Social Media Information

If you connect your account to social media platforms or register through them, we may collect information from those platforms in accordance with their privacy policies and your privacy settings on those platforms.

9. Communication Data

We collect information when you communicate with us, including customer support inquiries, feedback submissions, and survey responses. This helps us address your concerns and improve our services.

10. Third-Party Analytics

We use third-party analytics services to help us understand how our services are used. These services may collect information about your use of our website and other online services over time.

How We Use Your Information

11. Providing and Improving Services

We use your information to operate, maintain, and improve our platform, including developing new features, researching user trends, and optimizing content delivery. This ensures we can provide the best possible experience for our community.

12. Personalization

Your information helps us personalize your experience by showing relevant recipes, recommendations, and content based on your preferences, browsing history, and interactions with our platform.

13. Communication

We use your contact information to send important notices, respond to inquiries, provide customer support, and send newsletters or marketing communications (if you've opted in). You can opt out of marketing communications at any time.

14. Community Features

Your profile information and content submissions are used to facilitate community interactions, allowing other users to discover your recipes, follow your activity, and engage with your content as permitted by your privacy settings.

15. Security and Fraud Prevention

We use collected data to monitor for and prevent fraudulent activity, unauthorized access to accounts, and other security risks. This helps protect both our users and our platform from malicious actors.

16. Legal Compliance

We may use your information to comply with applicable laws, regulations, legal processes, or governmental requests, as well as to enforce our terms of service and protect the rights, property, or safety of Foodie Faves and our users.

17. Research and Development

Aggregated and anonymized data may be used for research purposes to understand cooking trends, dietary preferences, and other insights that help us improve our services and contribute to culinary knowledge.

18. Advertising

We may use your information to display relevant advertisements and measure their effectiveness. This includes both Foodie Faves promotions and third-party advertisements that may be of interest to our users.

How We Share Your Information

19. With Your Consent

We may share your information with third parties when we have your explicit consent to do so. For example, if you choose to connect your account to a social media platform or participate in a co-branded promotion.

20. Service Providers

We share information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our platform, such as hosting providers, payment processors, analytics services, and customer support tools. These providers are contractually obligated to protect your information.

21. Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, reorganization, bankruptcy, or other sale of assets, user information may be transferred as part of the transaction. We will notify you of any such change in ownership or control of your personal information.

22. Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, such as in response to a subpoena, court order, or other legal process, or if we believe disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, your safety, or the safety of others.

23. Aggregated or Anonymized Data

We may share aggregated or anonymized information that does not personally identify you with partners, researchers, or for other business purposes. This might include statistics about recipe popularity or dietary trends.

24. Community Interactions

Information you choose to share publicly (such as recipes, comments, or profile information) is visible to other users according to your privacy settings. We cannot control how other users may use this publicly available information.

Data Security

25. Protection Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These include encryption, access controls, and regular security audits.

26. Account Security

You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your account credentials. We recommend using strong, unique passwords and enabling two-factor authentication where available. Please notify us immediately of any unauthorized use of your account.

27. Data Breach Procedures

In the unlikely event of a data breach that affects your personal information, we will promptly investigate the incident and notify you and relevant authorities as required by applicable laws and regulations.

28. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to provide our services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. Inactive accounts may be deleted after a period of inactivity.

Your Rights and Choices

29. Access and Correction

You can access and update much of your personal information through your account settings. For additional requests, you may contact us to access, correct, or delete your personal information, subject to certain exceptions.

30. Data Portability

Upon request, we will provide you with a copy of your personal data in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format, where technically feasible.

31. Opting Out

You may opt out of receiving marketing communications from us by using the unsubscribe link in our emails or adjusting your notification preferences in your account settings. You cannot opt out of service-related communications.

32. Cookie Controls

Most web browsers allow you to manage cookie preferences through their settings. However, disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our website and services.

33. Do Not Track Signals

Our website does not currently respond to "Do Not Track" signals from browsers. However, we provide you with other tools to control how your data is collected and used, as described in this policy.

34. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected such information, we will take steps to delete it.

35. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We take appropriate safeguards to ensure your data remains protected when transferred internationally.

Additional Provisions

36. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these external sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

37. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of significant changes through our website or other communication channels. Your continued use of our services constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

38. California Privacy Rights

California residents have specific rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to request disclosure of data collection practices and opt out of the sale of personal information. Contact us for more information about these rights.

39. GDPR Compliance

For users in the European Economic Area, we comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). This includes providing additional rights regarding your personal data and ensuring lawful bases for processing your information.

40. Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@foodiefaves.com or through the contact information provided below.

Conclusion

At Foodie Faves, we take your privacy seriously and are committed to being transparent about our data practices. This Privacy Policy is designed to help you understand what information we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have regarding your personal data. We encourage you to review this policy periodically and contact us with any questions or concerns.
